No matter where in the world, the income distribution shares the same pattern.
Most people in the population earns much less than the 'Average Income'. The income of the extreme rich population filled the gap between the majority of people's actual income and the average income.

The 'Average Income' is bad in terms of reflecting how most people are doing. There are many other indicators such as 'Median Income', telling you the middle income of a population.
